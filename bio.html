About Me

While I got my degree in graphic design, photography will always be my one true love. I started experimenting with photography as early as I can remember, and studied it for two years at Parkland College. Film photography allows me to slow down and really think about each individual image, creating a sort of discipline. I love the way light and shadows play differently on film than they do through digital photography. I find inspiration almost everywhere, giving me a broad range of subjects.
